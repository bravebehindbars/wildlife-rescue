Partners And Other Resources

Our Mission: To provide evidence-based resources and education on wildlife rehabilitation to advance the field, promote wildlife welfare and conservation, and reduce human-wildlife conflicts.
Our mission: To stop the degradation of the earth's natural environment and to build a future in which humans live in harmony with nature by: conserving the world's biological diversity. ensuring that the use of renewable natural resources is sustainable.
Our mission: To rescue and protect animals around the world by: Reducing commercial exploitation of animals, Protecting wildlife habitats, Assisting animals in distress, Campaigning to prevent animal cruelty, and Restoring and protecting natural habitats.
Our Mission: WCS saves wildlife and wild places worldwide through science, conservation action, education, and inspiring people to value nature.
Our mission: To advance the safety and well-being of animals around the world. The organization's vision is for all animals to live free of suffering and cruelty, and its values include kindness, respect, compassion, awareness, and integrity.
NWRA is dedicated to improving the profession of wildlife rehabilitation and its contributions to preserving natural ecosystems. Their mission includes: Improving the care of wildlife in rehabilitation, Serving as a voice for the profession and Promoting the profession of wildlife rehabilitation.
International Bird Rescue's mission is to inspire people to act in balance with the natural world by rescuing waterbirds in crisis. They also aim to be a voice for birds in a changing global environment by engaging with the public to increase awareness.
The Global Wildlife Rescue Project's mission is to support wildlife rescue projects in developing countries and to show how people can live in harmony with the natural world. The project also states, "The wildlife and its habitat cannot speak, so we must and we will".
